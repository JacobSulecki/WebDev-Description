Front-End Web Developer

Education Required

With most technology related jobs, there will be at least some school involved. A front-end web developer is no exception most of the time. Though there are plenty of avenues out there, a lot of huge corporations still like to see a bachelors in computer science. I am happy to say though that especially recently, there are so many routes you can take such as a couple week bootcamps that get you up and running for your first junior position. Even some of these bootcamps go pretty in depth so pretty much most companies would take you in with a good enough interview.
